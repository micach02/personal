Personal Portfolio Website

Project Overview

This personal website serves as my digital portfolio, showcasing my skills, projects, and professional background. It is designed to provide visitors with an engaging overview of my work, technical expertise, and contact information.

Built with HTML5, CSS3, Bootstrap, and JavaScript, the site features a modern, responsive design and smooth navigation. It highlights selected projects, my technical stack, and a brief introduction about myself.

Role and Responsibilities

  • Designer & Developer: Designed and developed the entire website, including layout, color scheme, and interactive elements.
  • Content Creator: Wrote all content, including project descriptions, about section, and contact information.
  • Responsive Implementation: Ensured the site is fully responsive and accessible across devices.
  • Continuous Improvement: Regularly update the portfolio with new projects and skills.
